Avoidance of sovereign debt risk is fueling resurgent nationalism

German industrial exports. The Swiss franc. The British pound sterling. In their own way, each of these nations have moved to protect their real assets from being an ECB bargaining chip.

Other European nations are exploring sector-specific nationalism to protect themselves from each others sovereign debt risk.

Can the private equity backstop of this sovereign risk use trends in nationalism to protect itself?
