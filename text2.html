The State of the European Bond Market

Private equity is the backstop for Euro area sovereign debt risk.

From https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/key/date/2016/html/sp161103.en.html: “Euro area government bonds are not backed by any European authority.”

“The European Stability Mechanism cannot backstop every member's self-issued government bond obligation.”

“Euro area sovereign debt is exposed to credit risk.” “Therefore shock absorption within each member country has to take place either via the private sector, through financial risk-sharing, or by each national government individually through its fiscal policy.”

How can we invert the implied collateral of private equity into a performing asset?
